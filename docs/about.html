About

Study Lab is really two experienced software engineers working as a team to take on different projects for different clients.

Our credentials are: solid MS in Computer Science and Physics respectively, and 15+ years of experience each, in many different computer languages, tools and methodologies. We have worked for Alcatel, UCLA, and Caltech among others.

Taking full advantage of our current and coming information technologies, we work 100% remotely, usually from the US West Coast time zone. That said, we do not exclude the possibility of visiting a client if it can be a productive in person meeting.

In our opinion, this is the sensible way to work in this day and age: minimal commute, maximal geographical reach.
