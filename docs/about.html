About

Study Lab™ is a software engineering firm.

We work 100% remotely, usually from the US West Coast time zone.

That said, we do not exclude the possibility of visiting a client if it can be a productive meeting. In our opinion, that is the sensible way to work in this day and age: minimal commute, maximal geographical reach.

We started off as two consultants with MS in Computer Science and Physics and 15+ years of experience in many different computer languages, tools and methodologies. We have worked for Alcatel, UCLA, and Caltech among others.
